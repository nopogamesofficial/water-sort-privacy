Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026
App Name: Water Sort: Juice Puzzle
Developer: Nopo

This Privacy Policy describes how Water Sort: Juice Puzzle (the "App") handles your information.

1. Data Collection

The App does not require account creation, login, or any personally identifying information (such as your name, email address, or precise location) to function.

To help us fix bugs and improve the game, the App uses Google Firebase to collect limited, anonymized data, as described in Section 4. This data cannot be used to personally identify you.

2. Local Data

The App uses your device's local storage (Unity PlayerPrefs) to save:

  • Your game progress (current level, completed levels)
  • Your in-game settings (sound, music, haptics on/off)
  • Power-up inventory (undo, hint, shuffle counts)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Vibration — for haptic feedback during gameplay
  • Internet / Network access — to send anonymized analytics and crash reports to Google Firebase

No camera, microphone, precise location, or contacts permissions are required.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service provided by Google to collect limited, anonymized data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymized usage and interaction data (for example: levels played, session length, device model, operating system version, language, and approximate region derived from your IP address). Used to understand how the game is played and to improve it.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports and technical diagnostic data (such as device state, operating system version, and error logs) used to detect and fix bugs.

This data is processed by Google in accordance with its privacy policy. For more information, see:

  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

The App does not use any advertising networks or cross-app tracking services.

5. Children

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
